01 // About

Engineer. Researcher. Builder.

I'm a Graduate Research Assistant at Wright State University's Aerospace Systems Engineering program, working under Dean Darryl Ahner on eVTOL development within a Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) framework.

My work sits at the intersection of computational aircraft design, multidisciplinary optimization, and digital engineering — building the tools that enable next-generation Advanced Air Mobility.

From hover rotor analysis and battery energy budgets to V-tail authority checks, I build end-to-end parametric tools that close the loop between early concept and physics-validated design.
